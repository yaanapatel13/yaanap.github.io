5.) Fried Rice

Fifth on the list is fried rice. It is a dish that pairs well with noodles but can also be eaten by itself. This makes it the
perfect dish to make, regardless of your mood or the time of day it is. It is easy to make and can be enjoyed by a diverse
range of palates. Fried rice is rich in flavor and has a delightful aroma. You just can't go wrong with and can't mess up this Thai dish.

4.) Mac and Cheese

Baked mac and cheese is one of the best foods of all time. It makes you feel warm inside and makes everyone happy. It satisfies both
adults and children alike. It is very simple, but is also tasty and flavorful as the same time. Everyone loves mac and
cheese. There are so many possible varieties that can be made and suited to your own flavor profile.
